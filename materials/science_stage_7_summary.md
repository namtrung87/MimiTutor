# Cambridge Lower Secondary Science Stage 7 - Overview

## Unit 7: Microorganisms in the environment
- Key Concepts: Characteristics of living things, what microorganisms are (bacteria, viruses, fungi), how microorganisms interact with the environment.
- Scientific Vocabulary: Microorganism, bacteria, virus, fungi, decompose, decay.

## Unit 8: Changes to materials
- Key Concepts: Physical and chemical changes, states of matter (solid, liquid, gas), melting, freezing, boiling, condensing, dissolving, solutes, solvents, solutions.
- Scientific Vocabulary: State of matter, physical change, chemical change, reversible, irreversible, dissolve, solute, solvent, solution, saturation.

## Unit 9: Electricity
- Key Concepts: Simple electrical circuits, components of a circuit (cells, wires, bulbs, switches), series and parallel circuits, measuring current and voltage, conductors and insulators.
- Scientific Vocabulary: Circuit, component, cell, battery, wire, switch, bulb, current, ammeter, voltage, voltmeter, conductor, insulator.
